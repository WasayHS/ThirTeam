To move: Bring your mouse cursor one block above or beside the player (the player cannot move diagonally) 
The player can attack if they're beside, infront, behind the enemy. The player can also attack diagonally 
To attack, click on an enemy that is close to the player (behind, infront, beside, diag)
Go to the next level by going to the door/portal on the top of the grid, this door can only be opened after killing all the enemies
